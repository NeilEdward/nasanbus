# NasanBus

NasanBus is a platform designed to help commuters, drivers, and conductors communicate and monitor the real-time location of minibuses.

## Problem

Many minibus associations still rely on Messenger group chats and manual communication between drivers, conductors, and commuters. Passengers often have no visibility into where a minibus currently is or how long they need to wait. Drivers and conductors continuously exchange messages to inform each other about their current location, resulting in fragmented communication and inefficient coordination.

Common issues include:

* Commuters waiting without knowing when the next minibus will arrive.
* Drivers and conductors relying on chat messages instead of a dedicated tracking system.
* Difficulty determining which minibuses are currently active or available.
* Lack of real-time visibility for both passengers and operators.
* Delays and miscommunication caused by manual updates.

## Solution

NasanBus provides a centralized platform that enables:

* **Real-time minibus tracking** for commuters.
* **Location sharing** between drivers and conductors.
* **Improved communication** without relying solely on Messenger chats.
* **Estimated arrival awareness** for waiting passengers.
* **Better fleet visibility** for transport associations.

By digitizing location updates and communication, NasanBus aims to make public transportation more predictable, efficient, and convenient for everyone involved.

## Vision

To modernize local public transportation by connecting commuters and operators through a simple and accessible real-time tracking platform.
